Ther are not enough female role models. Women are underrepresented in the SECT fields. Early studies have shown horizontal and vertical segregation in SECT fields and barriered like socialisation,gender stereotypes and lack of role models creates the masculine image of technology and impact the fact that women had ~caring responsibilities~ adds to this. If computer science was so popular with females 100 years ago, why has it fallen out of favour now. I believe, it is the lack of female role models in this industry which serves as part of the culprit.
