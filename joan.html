Full Name Joan Elisabeth Lowther Clarke
Date Of Birth 24 June 1917 West Norwood, London, England, UK
Date Of Death 4 September 1996 (aged 79) Headington, Oxfordshire, England, UK
Education Newnham College, Cambridge
Mother Dorothy Elizabeth Fulford
Father Revd William Kemp Lowther Clarke

Background

Clarke was the youngest of 5 childeren and attended Newnham College, Cambridge gained a double first degree in maths.However, only full degrees at this time these were only awarded to males. Nonetheless, she was invited by Gordon Welchmann to join him in Governmnent Code and Cyber School breaking Geramn Enigma Code in Bletchley Park after being discovered in her undergraduate geometry class for her impecable mathematical skills.

Technological Contributions

She was placed in a all women group who did the clerical work as cryptology was not considered a job for women. Clarke was then placed in Hut 8 as the only female practioner of cryptanalytic process Banburismus reducing the work need for Bombes (an electromechanical device used to decipher German encrypted message). During her time at Bletchley, Clarke and Turing became very good friends until Turing's death 1954. The pair were inseparable, sharing the same hobbies, personalities spending nearly all their free time together and even synchronised their shifts. Turing asked for hand in marrigae however this ws then broken off when Turing confessed his homosexuality. She was promoted to Linguist eventhough she was not multilingual.

Why is she still relevant today?

Her efforts led to reduction of shipping be sunk via Wolf Pack(a Nazi naval tatic) from 282,00 tons to 62,000 tons in 1941. Clarke became deputy head of Hut 8 in 1944 however, she wasn't allowed to progress further due to gender inequality and the large pay gap between her and her male colleagues. After the war, Clarke worked at GCHQ where she met Lieutenant-Colonel John Kenneth Ronald Murray whom she married. Shortly afterwards, she took a break from GCHQ due to ill health then returned in 1962 where she remianed until 1977 age 60.
